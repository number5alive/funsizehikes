Eloise Robbins is a long distance adventurer who completed the Triple Crown of hiking in just over two years. She has hiked the Pacific Crest Trail, Continental Divide Trail, Appalachian Trail and Long Trail, in addition to many shorter distance treks. Eloise is passionate about human powered travel, and also enjoys canoeing, bikepacking, rock climbing and various forms of winter exploration. Eloise has lived in Scotland, Alaska, and now makes her home in Canada.

Eloise has worked as a writer, editor and communications manager for diverse companies, including book publishers, nonprofits and law firms. Her writing has appeared in multiple outdoor publications, including on Dirtbag Diaries and noted thru-hiking website The Trek.
